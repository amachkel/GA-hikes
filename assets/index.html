Georgia Hikes

Choose from the dropdown menu to display Georgia parks and their hiking trails. You can explore their difficulty levels that are color coded for your convenience, based on a formula that uses the length and trail elevation. When you make a selection, you will be presented with a 5 day forecast for that location.
